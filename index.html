Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from my clients

What industries do you specialize in?

I have extensive experience across multiple industries including technology, finance, healthcare, e-commerce, SaaS, and professional services. My research skills allow me to quickly become conversant in new industries as needed.

How do you determine pricing for projects?

Pricing is based on project scope, research requirements, word count, and turnaround time. I offer per-project pricing rather than hourly rates to provide budget certainty. Smaller projects start at $250, with comprehensive content strategies priced accordingly.

What's your typical turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days for most projects. Rush services (48-72 hour turnaround) are available for an additional 25% fee. Large projects like e-books or whitepapers typically require 2-3 weeks depending on research needs.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, all projects include up to two rounds of revisions at no additional cost. Additional revisions can be accommodated at my standard hourly rate. Revisions must be requested within 14 days of delivery.

How do you ensure content is original?

All content is written from scratch following extensive research. I use Copyscape Premium to verify originality before delivery, and can provide the report upon request. Plagiarism is never acceptable in professional writing.
